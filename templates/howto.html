How-To

Intelligent Deduper requires a list of family records with the correct headings in order to properly work. From left to right the columns must match exactly the following:

  • FamilyDUID
  • LastName
  • FirstNames
  • PrimaryAddress1
  • Primary Address 2
  • PrimaryCity
  • PrimaryState
  • PrimaryPostalCode
  • OwnerOrganizationID
  • OwnerOrganizationName
  • Owner Organization City
Once your import file is ready, go to the home page of Intelligent Deduper and upload the list.

After the file imports into the database you will be redirected to the training screen. Begin training Intelligent Deduper on what constitutes a duplicate record in your dataset.

Once enough possible matches have been indentified Intelligent Deduper can be run to search through and categorize possible duplicate records. Results can be downloaded once the process completes.
